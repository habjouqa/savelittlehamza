Hamza's Journey

There is hope. Elevidys is the first and only gene therapy approved for DMD, offering a groundbreaking treatment that could dramatically improve Hamza's quality of life. With your support, we can provide him with the life-saving gene therapy he needs to continue playing, smiling, and living his best life.
